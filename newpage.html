Question and Answer Page

Question: How long does a trip Usually last?
Answer: The Average trip lasts around an hour but you could ask for one of the more advanced cources that last about 30 minutes longer.

Question: How many People can I bring on the raft?
Answer: The maxium amount of people allowed on one raft is 6 people. If you have more you could ask for a second raft for a small price or you could reserve a raft if you want less than 6.

Question: How cold is the water?
Answer: This changes based on the time of year but it is an aveage of 75 degrees Fahrenheit

Question: What kind of saftey features are there on the raft?
Answer: Each raft is outfitted with harnesses that are checked at the end of each ride. Each person is also given a lifevest just in case someone falls out. You could alos request a helmet at no extra cost.

Question: How old do you have to be in order to go rafting?
Answer: The minimum age for rafting is 10 years old with adult supervision (16 and older). You must be 16 years old in order to go without supervision. And people over the age of 50 years old must get appoval from their doctor in order to go on the raft.

Question: What is your favorite color?
Answer: My favorite color is orange, and thank you for asking.
